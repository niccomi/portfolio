"The mistake most companies make is describing what they do rather than the context in which their product is the best choice."

## Key Takeaway

Positioning is not taglines. It's the **context** you place your product in so that the right people recognize it as the best option for them.

## The 5 Components

1. Competitive alternatives
2. Unique attributes
3. Value (and proof)
4. Target market characteristics
5. Market category
