The mind is like a ladder with finite rungs. You can't force your way in — you have to find a gap, or reposition the competition.

## The Ladder Theory

- Each category has a mental ladder
- The #1 spot holds ~40% of mindshare
- #2 holds ~20%
- #3 holds ~10%
- Everyone else fights for scraps

## How to Get On The Ladder

1. Be first (create the category)
2. Be the alternative to first
3. Narrow the focus
4. Reposition the leader
