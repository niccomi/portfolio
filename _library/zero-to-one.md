Every moment in business happens only once. Going from 0 to 1 means creating something genuinely new.

## The 0 to 1 vs 1 to n Distinction

| 0 to 1 | 1 to n |
|--------|--------|
| Vertical progress | Horizontal progress |
| Technology | Globalization |
| Monopoly | Competition |
| Secrets | Convention |

> "Competition is for losers."
