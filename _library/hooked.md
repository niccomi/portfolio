Trigger → Action → Variable Reward → Investment.

## The Hook Model

1. **Trigger** — External (notification) or internal (emotion)
2. **Action** — The simplest behavior in anticipation of reward
3. **Variable Reward** — Uncertainty increases dopamine
4. **Investment** — User puts in work, increasing future engagement

## Key Insight

Anticipation of the reward, not the reward itself, drives the loop.
